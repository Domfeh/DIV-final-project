DIAMOND RECYCLE CENTER

At Diamond Recycle Center,we recycle waste products.We believe that everyone can help make our country clean if they are given avenue and througth our initiatives people can sit in the comfort of their homes and make the clean Ghana we all desire a reality.

WHY WE DO IT

We aim to build a low cost waste collection infrastructure while raising general awareness on the importance of recycling for environmental sustainability and social welfare gained from reduction in pollution and diseases like malaria.

WHAT WE DO

Diamond Recycle Center offers a convenient household recycling service using a fleet of low cost cargo-bikes.We are powering social change using the enviroment by allowing people in low-income communities to capture value from their waste.

You can contact us for more information.Remember, a clean and beautiful Ghana begins with you and I.

MISSION AND VISION

OUR MISSION

To create indigenous collection schemes and educational campaigns that allows communities to harvest the full value of their waste.Support the growth of a circular economy by building the capacity of the informal sector in order to help them improve their livelihoods and the landscapes of the communities they serve.

OUR VISION
To become the authoritative voice on the creation of circular economies in West Africa,while enhancing and suporting goverments,diplomatic agencies and communities capacity to fully create and support a thriving ecosystem that harnesses the full value of waste.
